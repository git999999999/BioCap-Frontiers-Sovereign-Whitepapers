Global ESG reporting suffers from unverifiable inputs, incomplete datasets, artificial scoring, and greenwashing. The ESG truth ledger resolves this by accepting environmental data only from sovereign devices, ensuring that all environmental reporting reflects real-world conditions.

Each entry must correspond to an actual physical measurement, not a modeled prediction.
